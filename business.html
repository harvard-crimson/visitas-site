business

The Crimson Business Board raises revenue to support the annual operations of Harvard’s only daily publication and the oldest continuously published daily collegiate publication. The Crimson Business Board is the best way to gain real, valuable business experience as a student at Harvard. Our team is a lean and agile one, which allows every member of our team to solve complex business problems and make an impact on the growth of our business, all while working alongside some of the most genuine, passionate, and motivated people that you will meet.

Our business segments lie in: advertising, marketing services, educational programs, subscriptions, and contract printing, which allows us to work with a range of clients and partners, both domestically and abroad.

Unsurprisingly, the Business Board’s alumni include a variety of significant global leaders around the world in business and politics:

Steve Ballmer - Former CEO, Microsoft
Liz Hilton Segel - Managing Partner, McKinsey and Co., North America
Andy Jassy - CEO, Amazon Web Services
Magdalena Kala, VP, Bain Capital
John F. Kennedy - President of the United States
